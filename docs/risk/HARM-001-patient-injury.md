# Harm: Patient Injury from Incorrect Treatment

## Description

Patient experiences adverse health effects as a result of receiving incorrect treatment based on erroneous software output. Severity ranges from temporary discomfort to serious injury requiring medical intervention.

## Severity Assessment

**Rating: 4 (Major)**

- Potential for reversible but significant patient harm
- May require additional medical treatment
- Could result in extended hospital stay
- Not immediately life-threatening in most scenarios

## Harm Categories

- Medication overdose or underdose effects
- Delayed treatment due to incorrect information
- Adverse drug interactions from wrong medication
- Physiological stress from incorrect procedures

## Regulatory Implications

- Reportable adverse event under MDR requirements
- Potential FDA MedWatch filing
- CAPA investigation required
