# Harm: Regulatory Enforcement Action Against Organization

## Description

FDA or other regulatory body takes enforcement action against the organization due to compliance failures in the quality management system, potentially resulting in warning letters, consent decrees, or market restrictions.

## Severity Assessment

**Rating: 3 (Moderate)**

- Significant business impact but recoverable
- Reputation damage with customers and partners
- Financial penalties and remediation costs
- No direct patient harm from this specific harm

## Harm Categories

- Warning letters requiring response
- 483 observations during inspections
- Consent decree restrictions on operations
- Product recall or market withdrawal requirements

## Business Impact

- Estimated remediation cost: $100K - $1M
- Timeline impact: 6-18 months to resolve
- Customer trust: Moderate impact on sales
